CONFIDENCE THROUGH COMPETITION

23 NOVEMBER 2017

I recently took an opportunity to compete at a local competition. Although I have been avoiding competition, something inside me knew it had to be done. Through nervousness and fear I resisted competition untill October the 22nd. That day would change my life even though lost both of my matches; that was not important to me. My team, my friends, my family and my coach showed mad support. Conquereing the fear and anxiety of competition permiated through my entire soul and squashed so many other anxieties I experience daily that I had no control over. The most important lesson of that competition was recognizing my anxiety and being able to control it. I have increased my self confidence in high stress situations, this is an invaluable skill. Not only would I recommend competition buy I encourage it.
